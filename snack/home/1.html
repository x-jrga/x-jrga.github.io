Welcome to Snack

Understand your food intake


Snack is learning software for nutrition, a meal design tool and calculator that could facilitate achieving your health goals and objectives by first helping you understand your food intake and second by allowing you to create new food combinations that meet specific requirements.

Snack is a training program designed to find lowest calorie food combination. It lets you experiment and get faster, better, less expensive results so you can rapidly learn. Its intended audience is anyone with interest in nutrition.

My goal was to improve my diet and health so I wrote this software program to do it. It did. I must say my initial education in food science and nutrition made things easier. Paying attention to the dietary recommendations made it possible.

This software is now available to the whole world (7.674 billion people) for free.

I expect it to get analyzed, tested, copied, customized, improved and recreated.

I know it will add a little value.
