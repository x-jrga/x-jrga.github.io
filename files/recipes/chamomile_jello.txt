Chamomile Jello

Ingredients:

1) Two chamomile tea bags
2) 2 cups of water
3) 10 grams of Psyllium Husk Powder
4) 10 sucralose (Splenda) packets

To Prepare:

1. Brew two cups of chamomile tea
2. Mix 10 grams of Psyllium Husk Powder and 10 Splenda packets
3. Add hot tea to powder mix and stir well
4. Place in fridge and wait
5. Eat it like a gelatin dessert 

Use if there is not enough fiber in your diet. It is soluble fiber and it helps 
your digestive system perform better.
