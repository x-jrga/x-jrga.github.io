Snack: Learning Software For Nutrition

Snack is learning software for nutrition, a meal design tool and calculator that could facilitate achieving your health goals and objectives by first helping you understand your food intake and second by allowing you to create new food combinations that meet specific requirements. It is also training program designed to find lowest calorie food combination. It lets you experiment and get faster, better, less expensive results so you can rapidly learn.



PotatoSql: Learning Software For Database Design

PotatoSql allows me to create database schemas with minimal effort and to quickly generate corresponding sql source code for Hsqldb including referential integrity rules.
